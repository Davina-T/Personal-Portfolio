About Us


The Kai & Hiro online store was started by parents for parents. Kai & Hiro is not only an online store but also a Brand Name. It was registered on IP Australia in 2018. The idea for the name came from my beloved son, Kai, and my dearest pet, Hiro. We call them The Two Brothers! Since I adopted Hiro while I was pregnant in 2016, Hiro is the elder brother.

We love our brand as much as we love Kai & Hiro. We are putting lots of effort into building the brand, knowing it will take time. Even though we only have one product (Crib Rail Cover) so far, we believe that we will have many more in the future.
